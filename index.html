What We Do

Bouldering & English, Together

Eigo Climbing is a one-of-a-kind programme combining bouldering instruction with natural English language learning. Sessions are fun, physical, and fully immersive — students learn as they climb.

  • 🏔️Real climbing skills — technique, footwork, movement, and safety taught by qualified instructors.
  • 🇬🇧Native English instruction — vocabulary, commands, and conversation woven naturally into every session.
  • 🧠Learning through movement — physical activity boosts language retention and cognitive engagement.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧All ages welcome — tailored programmes from young beginners (age 5) through to adults.

Our Programmes

Find Your Session

Every session runs for one hour and blends bouldering with English. Choose the level that suits you best.

Ages 5 – 10

Little Climbers

Playful, games-based sessions where young children discover the wall and English simultaneously. Focus on colours, directions, body parts, and simple commands — all through movement.

🕐 60 minutes  ·  🧒 Max 6 per session
Ages 11 – 17

Youth Ascent

Build real technique on the boulder wall while expanding English vocabulary for sport, travel, and everyday life. Problem-solving and team challenges included.

🕐 60 minutes  ·  🧒 Max 8 per session
Adults 18+

Summit English

Conversational English in an active setting. Great for professionals or anyone looking to improve confidence in English while developing a new outdoor skill.

🕐 60 minutes  ·  🧑 Max 8 per session
